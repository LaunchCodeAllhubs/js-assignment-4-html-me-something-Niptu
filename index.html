Sharlotka (Russian Apple Pie)

Russian Sharlotka is a light sponge cake filled with small apple pieces. It’s a simple dessert made with very inexpensive ingredients which most people always have on hand.


COOK TIME: 40 minutes

Ingredients:
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 3 apples
  • butter
  • cinnamon if you like ;)

    • Instruction:

    Whipped eggs with sugar, add baking powder and flour, beat thoroughly. Peel apples from skin and seeds, cut into thin slices. Put the apples in a form greased with butter, pour over the dough and bake for 30-40 minutes at 355°F.
